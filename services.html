Residential and Commercial Waste Collection

We provide tailored waste collection schedules for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Our services include weekly pickups, on-demand collections, and flexible container options. Our team ensures punctual, reliable, and safe collection practices.

Advanced Recycling Programs

GreenWorks offers comprehensive recycling programs that cover paper, plastics, metals, glass, and electronics. We provide educational materials, customized reporting, and tracking systems to help clients monitor their recycling performance and reduce landfill impact.

Organic Waste Solutions

Our organic waste services transform food scraps, yard trimmings, and biodegradable waste into high-quality compost. These programs support urban gardens, farms, and community green projects, promoting circular resource use.

Hazardous Waste Management

We handle hazardous materials with the highest safety standards. Our disposal solutions include chemicals, batteries, paints, and e-waste. Each procedure complies with local and national regulations to prevent environmental harm.

Consulting and Sustainability Planning

Our consulting services help clients develop sustainable waste management strategies. From route optimization and carbon reporting to circular economy solutions, GreenWorks provides guidance to improve environmental outcomes while optimizing operational efficiency.
